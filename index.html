Ordinary HS life

You're a freshmen in highschool who is alone, after a bit of wandering around, you find your classroom. You enter the classroom and take a seat, shortly after, you got called on to introduce yourself.


In-class

You're in class, in your seat. Someone approaches you, and it happens to be someone who shares the same interests as you. What do you do?

You pretended to be asleep

Turns out the person that tried greeting you was the most popular kid at school, and since you ignored them, he'll make your life at school a living hell

You greeted them

You greeted the person who came up to you, and turns out, they were the most popular kid at school. They want to be friends with you, however, you're a bit reluctant. What do you do?

You rejected him

The popular kid got ticked off and left, saying that he'll get you back for this. You wonder what's going to happen, but you think nothing of it.

You agreed to being his friend

The popular kid became really happy because he got a new friend, although you don't know how to feel about it.

You rejected him (p2)

It's the end of the day and you want go leave, but you get stopped by the popular kid and his gang. You immediately get intimidated and asks what he wants. He doesn't say anything except turn his back on you.

You agreed to being his friend (p2)

Since you're the friend of the popular kid, he expects you to do everything he says, or else [we can leave that up to your imagination]

Death.
